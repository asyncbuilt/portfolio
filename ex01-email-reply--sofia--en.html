Before / After
Before — manual reply
Hello Julie,

Thank you for your message and for your interest in my work.

Regarding your date, October 18th, I will check my availability and get back to you shortly.

In terms of pricing, my packages start from €1,200 for 4 hours and go up to €2,800 for a full day. For 6 hours of coverage, I would suggest a package around €2,000.

Please let me know if you have any questions.

Best regards,
Sofia
Generic. No personalisation. No recommendation. Writing time: 8–10 min.
After — AI-generated reply
Julie, what a beautiful setting for a wedding — a countryside venue with an intimate group sounds like exactly the kind of day that's full of genuine moments.

October 18th looks good on my end, so that's a relief! I'd love to be there.

For 6 hours of coverage, the package that would fit you best is my Classic — it includes 8 hours (so you'd have a comfortable buffer), 400 edited photos, and a private online gallery. It's €2,000 in total, with a 30% deposit to lock in the date.

That said, if 4 hours would cover everything, my Essential at €1,200 is also worth considering — though for a day that flows from ceremony to evening, I usually find the extra time makes a real difference.

The way I work is pretty relaxed — I follow the day as it unfolds and just try to catch the real stuff. Small weddings with 40 guests are honestly some of my favorites.
Personalised, warm, recommendation matched to the request. Time: under 2 min.
